Brandy-Roasted Chestnuts

Descriptoion

Plane street food found in the streets of the North

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. chestnuts (roughly 35 or so)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 Tbs. honey
  • 1 cup brandy
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Steps

  1. Carve an X into the flat side of each chestnut with a sharp knife.
  2. Boil for 30 seconds.
  3. Drain and move to an oven-safe pan.
  4. Roast in oven at 425F for about 25-30 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool until handleable, then peel.
  6. Move the peeled chestnuts to a frying pan, sprinkle with the sugar and honey.
  7. Add brandy and warm over medium heat.
  8. Using a long match or lighter, light the brandy.
  9. Shake the pan slightly, keeping on the heat, until the brandy burns out.
  10. Add the cinnamon, and continue to cook, stirring occasionally to coat the chestnuts, until the mixture thickens somewhat into a syrup.
  11. Serve warm.
